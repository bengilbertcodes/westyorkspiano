Welcome to The West Yorkshire Piano Studio!

Hi!

I'm Ben and I've been running the West Yorkshire Piano Studio in Crossgates since 2018.

It was set up to offer top-class, family-friendly music lessons; and performing and recording opportunities on our gorgeous Steinway Model S.

I've been playing the piano since before I can remember and have over 20 years teaching experience, have performed with many bands and accompanied soloists at venues across the UK and beyond.

So if you're looking for music lessons, a skilled accompanist, live music for any event or to hire our piano studio for rehearsals and recordings, you've come to the right place!

Lessons are availble for all ages and abilities; whether you want to play classical masterpieces, improve those jazz chops, write your own songs or belt out your favorite pop tunes, the West Yorkshire Piano Studio has something for everyone.

Please use the menu above and explore more of what we do and get in touch if you have any questions about what the West Yorkshire Piano Studio can do to help you on your musical journeys.
